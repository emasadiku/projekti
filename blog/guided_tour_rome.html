4-Day Rome Itinerary

Dates for February: 7,14,21

Price: 250€

Day 1: Ancient Rome and Iconic Landmarks

Morning: Colosseum and Roman Forum

Begin your Roman adventure with a visit to the iconic Colosseum, one of the greatest works of Roman architecture. Then, head to the Roman Forum to explore the ruins of ancient government buildings and temples.

Afternoon: Palatine Hill

After the Roman Forum, climb Palatine Hill for spectacular views of the city and learn about Rome’s ancient history. It’s the perfect spot for panoramic photos.

Evening: Trevi Fountain and Spanish Steps

In the evening, visit the famous Trevi Fountain, where you can throw a coin for luck. Then, stroll to the Spanish Steps, a popular meeting point and one of Rome's most iconic landmarks.

Day 2: Vatican City and Artistic Wonders

Morning: St. Peter’s Basilica

Start your day at St. Peter’s Basilica, the heart of the Catholic Church. Admire the stunning architecture and Michelangelo’s famous Pietà statue. Don’t miss the chance to climb to the dome for breathtaking views of Vatican City.

Afternoon: Vatican Museums and Sistine Chapel

Explore the Vatican Museums, which house an immense collection of art, including Raphael’s Rooms and the renowned Sistine Chapel. The chapel’s ceiling, painted by Michelangelo, is one of the most famous artworks in the world.

Evening: Castel Sant’Angelo

End your day with a visit to Castel Sant’Angelo, an ancient fortress and museum. It offers beautiful views of the Tiber River and is especially magical at sunset.

Day 3: Charming Neighborhoods and Local Culture

Morning: Piazza Navona

Start your day at Piazza Navona, one of Rome's most beautiful squares, known for its Baroque architecture and the stunning Fountain of the Four Rivers. It’s a great place to have a coffee at one of the outdoor cafes.

Afternoon: Pantheon and Piazza del Popolo

Visit the Pantheon, an ancient Roman temple turned church, and marvel at its massive domed roof. Afterward, head to Piazza del Popolo, one of the most beautiful squares in Rome.

Evening: Trastevere Neighborhood

For a taste of local Roman life, explore the charming Trastevere neighborhood. Wander through its narrow streets, discover hidden piazzas, and enjoy dinner in one of the traditional trattorias.

Day 4: Relax and Enjoy the Beauty of Rome

Morning: Villa Borghese Gardens

Start your day with a relaxing walk through the Villa Borghese Gardens, Rome’s central park. You can also rent a bike or a rowboat to explore the area further.

Afternoon: Galleria Borghese

Visit the Galleria Borghese, a world-renowned art museum located in the Villa Borghese. The gallery showcases masterpieces by Bernini, Caravaggio, and many other renowned artists.

Evening: Piazza Venezia and Capitoline Hill

Finish your trip by visiting Piazza Venezia, one of Rome's busiest squares. From here, take a short walk to Capitoline Hill for fantastic views of the Roman Forum and the city below.
