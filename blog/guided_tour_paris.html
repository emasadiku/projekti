4-Day Paris Itinerary

Dates for February: 6, 13, 20

Price: 300€

Day 1: Explore the Heart of Paris

Morning: Eiffel Tower

Start your day by visiting the world-famous Eiffel Tower. Enjoy breathtaking views of Paris from the top and take some memorable photos.

Afternoon: Champs-Élysées and Arc de Triomphe

Stroll down the iconic Champs-Élysées, one of the most famous avenues in the world. End the walk at the Arc de Triomphe, where you can climb to the top for panoramic views of the city.

Evening: Seine River Cruise

Take a relaxing boat cruise along the Seine River to see Paris illuminated at night. Pass by iconic landmarks such as Notre-Dame, the Louvre, and the Eiffel Tower.

Day 2: Immerse Yourself in Art and Culture

Morning: Louvre Museum

Visit the Louvre, home to thousands of works of art including the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo. Take your time exploring the museum’s vast collections.

Afternoon: Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur

Explore the charming Montmartre district, known for its bohemian atmosphere and artistic history. Climb to the top of the hill to visit the stunning Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Evening: Moulin Rouge Show

Enjoy a spectacular cabaret show at the famous Moulin Rouge. This iconic Parisian venue is known for its dazzling performances and vibrant atmosphere.

Day 3: Historical and Iconic Landmarks

Morning: Notre-Dame Cathedral

Start your day with a visit to the Notre-Dame Cathedral. Admire its gothic architecture and the beautiful stained-glass windows.

Afternoon: Musée d'Orsay

Visit the Musée d'Orsay, housed in a former railway station, to see an impressive collection of impressionist and post-impressionist masterpieces.

Evening: Dinner at Le Marais

End your day with a delicious dinner in the Le Marais district. This charming neighborhood is filled with trendy cafes, restaurants, and bars.

Day 4: Relax and Shop in Style

Morning: Luxembourg Gardens

Take a leisurely stroll through the beautiful Luxembourg Gardens, one of the most serene and picturesque parks in Paris.

Afternoon: Shopping at Galeries Lafayette

Spend your afternoon shopping at the iconic Galeries Lafayette. This famous department store offers a wide range of luxury brands and fashion boutiques.

Evening: Sunset at Tuileries Garden

End your Paris adventure with a peaceful evening walk through the Tuileries Garden, a historic garden with beautiful views of the Louvre and the Place de la Concorde.
