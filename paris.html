Explore Paris

Top Attractions in Paris

  • Eiffel Tower: An iconic structure offering stunning views of the city.
  • Louvre Museum: Home to the famous Mona Lisa and other masterpieces.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral: A gothic architectural marvel.
  • Sainte-Chapelle: A beautiful chapel known for its stained glass windows.
  • Montmartre: Known for its artistic vibe and Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Where to Eat

Don't miss out on French delicacies such as croissants, escargot, and crepes!

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Paris is from April to June or October to early November when the weather is pleasant, and the city is less crowded.
